Current commands:
!summoner SummonerName
- Shows the summoner name, the summoner level and the summoner icon.

!mastery SummonerName
- Shows the summoner name, the champion that this user has most mastery points, the mastery level,
the champion icon and the last time that champion was played by that summoner.

!freeweek
- Shows a list of the champios available for free on the current week.
Each item of the list has the champion name, the title, roles, and basic info, alongside with the
champion icon.
